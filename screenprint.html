My Screenprinting Project

For this technology project, I designed and created a custom graphic to screenprint onto a physical surface. The process started with developing a clean digital design, making sure the lines were bold and distinct so they would transfer properly onto the screen mesh.

Project Details & Process

Once the design was ready, it was transferred onto a screen using a stencil method to block out the areas where ink shouldn't go. I carefully lined up my material, applied fabric ink across the top of the screen, and used a squeegee with firm pressure to pull the ink down, transferring the design perfectly onto the surface.

Key Materials & Steps

  • Custom digital graphic design stencil
  • Screenprinting frame and fine mesh screen
  • Screenprinting ink and a rubber squeegee
  • The final physical material/surface for printing
